# Decisions — Atelier Fuego

## D-001 | Hero Seed Selection | Design
- **Choice:** `S-AmbientAtmosphere` as primary hero anchor with an editorial typography mutation layer.
- **Why:** Preserves cinematic depth and avoids the already-overused split-agency hero.
- **Risk:** Can degrade into an atmospheric blur wall if the visual layers stay too close in luminance.

## D-002 | Navigation Direction | Design
- **Choice:** `N-Contextual` to preserve theme shifts across dark/light/accent sections.
- **Why:** It supports a stronger brand-driven motion system than a static pill.

## D-003 | Retry Policy | Quality
- **Choice:** Auto-retry the hero until depth and craft both land at MEDIUM+.
- **Why:** The current hero is close enough to threshold that human intervention would be premature.
